About The Innovator Amer Abdullah

Transforming Ethiopian Healthcare with AI

Amer Abdullah is a visionary **Ethiopian innovator** and technology entrepreneur deeply committed to revolutionizing healthcare delivery across Ethiopia through **cutting-edge AI-powered solutions**. As the **founder of Raji Technology**, he has successfully developed and deployed multiple intelligent systems that have garnered significant national recognition.

His cornerstone projects, the Integrated Smart Healthcare Solutions (ISHCS) and the Smart Nurse Calling System, represent significant advancements. Both have been personally presented to **Prime Minister Dr. Abiy Ahmed Ali** and officially endorsed by the **Ethiopian Ministry of Innovation and Technology**, highlighting their national importance and impact on public health.

To leverage advanced technology, particularly AI, to make quality healthcare more accessible, efficient, and equitable for all Ethiopians.

Vision: Preventable Healthcare Challenges Eliminated

To create a future where preventable healthcare challenges are significantly reduced or eliminated through smart, integrated technological interventions, improving lives nationwide.

Amer Abdullah's Government-Endorsed Innovations

Integrated Smart Healthcare Solutions (ISHCS)

The **ISHCS** is a cutting-edge, **AI-powered comprehensive healthcare platform** developed by Raji Technology, proudly endorsed by the Ethiopian Ministry of Innovation and Technology. It aims to digitalize and streamline various aspects of healthcare delivery across the nation.

Key Features of ISHCS:

  • **AI-powered diagnostic assistance** for faster, more accurate medical assessments, aiding clinicians.
  • **Real-time patient monitoring** to ensure continuous care and alert medical staff to critical changes instantly.
  • Efficient **digital health records management (EHR)**, improving accessibility, data integrity, and interoperability.
  • Integrated **smart nurse communication system** to optimize response times and improve patient-nurse interactions.
  • **Telemedicine capabilities** extending vital healthcare access to remote and underserved areas of Ethiopia.

Government Endorsement

Officially recognized and supported by the Ethiopian Ministry of Innovation and Technology on April 24, 2025, validating its importance for national healthcare infrastructure and digital transformation.

Smart Nurse Calling System

This **copyrighted patient communication system** is designed to revolutionize nurse-patient interaction within healthcare facilities, significantly enhancing patient safety, comfort, and staff efficiency.

Key Benefits & Features:

  • Achieves a remarkable **40% reduction in nurse response times**, ensuring prompt and critical patient care.
  • Features **wireless call buttons** with **priority alerts**, allowing patients to easily summon help based on urgency.
  • Includes a **centralized monitoring dashboard** for nurses to efficiently manage and prioritize patient requests.
  • **Automated logging** of call times and responses for improved accountability and performance tracking.

Copyright Registered

Protected under the Ethiopian Intellectual Property Authority with Registration No. 5/0277 on July 19, 2023, confirming its unique intellectual property status and innovation.
