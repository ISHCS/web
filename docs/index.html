About The Innovator

Transforming Healthcare Through Innovation

Amer Abdullah is an Ethiopian innovator and technology entrepreneur dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare delivery through cutting-edge solutions. As the founder of Raji Technology, he has developed multiple AI-powered systems that have received national recognition and government endorsement.

His flagship projects include the Integrated Smart Healthcare Solutions (ISHCS) and the Smart Nurse Calling System, both of which have been presented to Prime Minister Dr. Abiy Ahmed Ali and endorsed by the Ministry of Innovation and Technology.

Mission

To leverage technology for accessible, efficient healthcare across Ethiopia

Vision

A future where technology eliminates preventable healthcare challenges

Government-Endorsed Innovations

Integrated Smart Healthcare Solutions (ISHCS)

The ISHCS is a comprehensive AI-powered healthcare platform endorsed by the Ethiopian Ministry of Innovation and Technology. This system addresses critical challenges in Ethiopia's healthcare infrastructure through technological integration.

Key Features:

  • AI-powered diagnostic assistance
  • Real-time patient monitoring
  • Automated resource allocation
  • Digital health records management
  • Smart nurse communication system

Government Endorsement

Officially recognized by the Ministry of Innovation and Technology on April 24, 2025, as a nationally significant healthcare innovation.

Smart Nurse Calling System

A copyrighted patient communication system (Registration No. 5/0277) that revolutionizes nurse-patient interaction in healthcare facilities, reducing response times and improving patient outcomes.

System Benefits:

  • 40% reduction in nurse response times
  • Wireless call buttons with priority alerts
  • Centralized monitoring dashboard
  • Mobile notifications for staff
  • Detailed response analytics

Copyright Registered

Ethiopian Intellectual Property Authority, Registration No. 5/0277 on July 19, 2023.
