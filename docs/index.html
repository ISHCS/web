About Me

As the founder of Raji Technology, I am dedicated to pioneering innovative solutions that address critical challenges in healthcare and beyond. My journey is driven by a passion for leveraging technology to create tangible, positive impacts on society, and I am actively involved in incubating other innovations for the country.

My work primarily focuses on integrating advanced systems and AI-powered solutions to enhance patient care, safety, and operational efficiency within health facilities. I believe in a future where technology makes healthcare more accessible, efficient, and patient-centric for everyone.

I have had the distinct honor of presenting my innovations to esteemed government figures, including His Excellency Prime Minister Dr. Abiy Ahmed Ali, and key ministers, underscoring the national importance and recognition of my contributions.

My Innovations

Integrated Smart Healthcare Solutions (ISHCS)

The ISHCS project is a comprehensive system designed to revolutionize healthcare delivery. It integrates multiple advanced technologies to improve patient outcomes and operational efficiency.

  • Enhanced Patient-Provider Communication
  • Electronic Health Records (EHR)
  • Real-Time Location Systems (RTLS)
  • AI-Powered Solutions for data-driven decision-making
  • Reduced Medication Errors through CPOE
  • Potential Reduction in Hospital Mortality Rates

Endorsed by the Ministry of Innovation and Technology, aligning with national priorities.

Nurse Calling System

A core component of ISHCS, this innovative Nurse Calling System improves patient safety and staff responsiveness. It's designed to streamline communication between patients and healthcare providers.

  • Instant Patient-to-Nurse Communication
  • Mobile Notifications for Staff
  • Data Analytics on Response Times
  • Improved Patient Safety and Satisfaction

Copyright registered with the Ethiopian Intellectual Property Office (EIPO) in 2023.
